Glitter Gallery

The Open Source Social Platform for Design Collaboration and Commentary

Control

Built in version control gives you a full view of the history of your work - letting you easily track and rollback all of your changes.

Comment

Share your work and collect feedback in a single thread. Mark actionable comments as issues you can track.

Collaborate

Fork a project and make it your own, or pull other designers' changes into your own project.

Contribute

Glitter Gallery is 100% Open Source and easy to host yourself. Fork us on GitHub and help us make it better!
